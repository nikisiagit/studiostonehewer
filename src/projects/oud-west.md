intro_text_2: >-
  Nestled in the heart of upper Oud-West Amsterdam, this 1930s apartment stands
  as a testament to enduring elegance. For our first-time buyers, the mission
  was clear: to honour the mid-century character of the neighbourhood while
  crafting a curated sanctuary they could proudly call home. Infusing the space
  with a signature Studio Stonehewer flair, we embraced this by selecting a
  series of empowering and bold design choices, a contrasting range of fabrics
  and furnishings, and one-of-a-kind vintage pieces.


  Throughout the open-plan living area and bedrooms, locally sourced treasures like an 'Amsterdamse School' side table and a weathered-brass bar cart add character and authenticity.


  Geometric prints in rich ochre and ocean blue hues juxtapose beautifully with organic materials, such as a large rush-woven rug or a hand-thread wool blanket, fostering an ambiance that is both warm and luxuriously cozy.


  Every detail reflects the original story, creating a space that is unapologetically unique, designed to be lived in, shared, and loved for years to come.
